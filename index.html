Relaxation Center For University Students

Do you ever feel the need to relax on a regular day at college and can't find a place to rest ?

Everyone needs to relax once in a while in order to gain back that energy and focus which one has at the start of the day. Students need to do this too! However, there isn't any place on any university campuses where students can actually relax and by actually relax I mean like taking a nap. All the good universities have various provisions for the students for entertainment and fun but none for relaxation. So the problem is where do students relax if they intend to spend a lot of time on the campus?

During a course at University of Florida, Gainesville we (a group of classmates) came up with an idea to solve this problem:

We came up with a floor plan for a building inside any university which can be converted into a relaxation center where students can come and relax, take a nap, meditate or even study in a peaceful and comforting environment.
